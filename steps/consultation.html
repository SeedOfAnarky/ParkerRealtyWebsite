Step 1: Initial Consultation

The initial consultation is a crucial first step in your home buying journey. This free, no-obligation meeting allows us to get to know each other, discuss your home buying goals, and create a personalized strategy for your search.

A relaxed, informative consultation helps set clear expectations for your home buying journey.

What To Expect During Your Consultation

Getting To Know You

I'll ask questions to understand your lifestyle, preferences, and priorities to build a clear picture of your ideal home.

Financial Readiness Review

We'll discuss budget considerations, financing options, and help you understand what price range is realistic for your situation.

Tucson Market Overview

I'll share insights about current market conditions, trending neighborhoods, and what to expect in today's competitive environment.

The $1 Promise Explanation

I'll explain how my unique commission structure works and why I'm confident in my ability to negotiate on your behalf.

Creating Your Action Plan

Together, we'll develop a customized strategy and timeline for your home search based on your needs and the current market.

How to Prepare for Your Consultation

To make our initial meeting as productive as possible, consider bringing the following information:

  • Must-Have Features: A list of non-negotiable features your new home needs to have
  • Nice-to-Have Features: Amenities that would be a bonus but aren't deal-breakers
  • Preferred Neighborhoods: Areas of Tucson you're interested in (or if you're relocating, the type of community you prefer)
  • Timeline: Your ideal timeframe for finding and moving into a new home
  • Pre-Approval Letter: If you've already spoken with a lender (if not, I can recommend trusted partners)
  • Questions: Any concerns or questions you have about the process

Consultation Options

I offer flexible consultation formats to suit your schedule and comfort level:

  • In-Person: Meet at my office in Tucson for a face-to-face conversation
  • Video Call: Connect virtually via Zoom, FaceTime, or your preferred platform
  • Phone Call: If you're short on time, we can start with a focused phone consultation

Consultations typically last 45-60 minutes, but I'm happy to spend as much time as needed to ensure all your questions are answered.

Parker's Pro Tips

Get Pre-Approved Before Home Shopping

In today's competitive Tucson market, having a pre-approval letter ready before we start viewing homes gives you a significant advantage. Sellers take pre-approved buyers more seriously, and it helps us move quickly when you find the perfect home. I'm happy to connect you with trusted local lenders who can make this process smooth and painless.

Create a "Home Buying Wishlist" Spreadsheet

Before our consultation, consider creating a simple spreadsheet with columns for "Must-Have," "Nice-to-Have," and "Absolute Deal-Breakers." This exercise helps clarify your priorities and ensures we don't waste time looking at properties that don't meet your needs. Bring this to our consultation, and we'll refine it together.
